AI-Powered Mini Banner GIF & Logo Ad System to help small content creators

Introduction

We introduce a new, non-disturbing advertising format—mini animated banner GIFs and brand logos placed smartly on creator videos,images, powered by an AI system that matches advertisers with the perfect content creators.

  1. Helps adverstising agencies to reach a larger audience
  2. Small creators get the opportunity to earn

The Problems that small content cretors face

  • Long time needed to reach monetisation

  • Low and Unfair Payment

  • Lack of Visibility & Growth

The Innovative Idea

Our solution introduces a creative advertising model

Mini Banner GIFs Above the Video

  • A small animated GIF banner appears at the top of the Reel/Short
  • Promotes a brand without interrupting the main content

Logo or Poster on the Video/Post

  • A small brand logo or poster appears subtly on the video
  • Blends with the content so the viewer keeps watching
